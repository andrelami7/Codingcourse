Our mission
To introduce a fusion of basque landscape paintings with basque ancient tapestry methods

Loli and Manu were raised by the windy and picturesque shores of Basque Country of Northern Spain.

Loli is a tapestry maker, using one of the oldest practices in the country.

Manu is a painter, inspired by the beautiful landscapes of the country.
